Have your Vegass always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Vegas Wallet is the first mobile Vegas app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Vegas amount in VGC and mVGC.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Vegas via NFC, QR-codes or Vegas URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Vegas addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Vegas balance.

You can find more about Vegas at
http://www.vegass.org

If you want to contribute to Vegas Wallet, the project is located at
